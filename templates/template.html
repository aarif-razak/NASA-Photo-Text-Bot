NASA Astro Photo of the Day 'Bot'

An experiment in Flask, APIs, Python, and Heroku, I present my NASA Photo Astro Photo of the Day Bot.

It communicates with the Twilio API and NASA's APIs to effectively send a photo to your phone. The full list of photos can been seen here.

Any date from today through June 16th, 1995 *should* return a valid photo.

Instructions:

Text: 928-433-4317

  • A date in the format of YYYY-MM-DD

For NASA's astronomy photo of the day!

NOTE: if there is no response, this is likely the Heoku App falling asleep. I'll get an email and redploy it ASAP!
